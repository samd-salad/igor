# Wake-word training experiments

Each section below is one run of `wakeword/diagnostic_run.py`. Sections are appended in chronological order. The **Verdict** picks the epoch with the lowest holdout FP at the runtime threshold (>0.95) *among epochs where positive recall is intact* (pos_min ≥ 0.5).

> **Diagnostic methodology:** train 100 epochs on the full augmented dataset, snapshot model state every 10 epochs, then re-score the 850-clip held-out negative split at each snapshot. Holdout split is file-level, deterministic (seed=4317, 20% of negatives), and never seen during training. pos_min and pos_mean are measured on the 1730 augmented training-positive windows; FP counts are on the 850 holdout clips (each scored with stride=1 to capture peak score across any window position). top_firer is the highest score any holdout clip achieved (max=1.000 means the model is maximally wrong on at least one clip — architectural ceiling signal).
